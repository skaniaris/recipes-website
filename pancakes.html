Old Fashioned Pancakes

Description

This fluffy and cozy pancake recipe creates a thick, homestyle, buttery pancake. A melt in your mouth taste perfectly paired with a maple syrup. Topped with a pat of butter for a filling breakfast.


Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purupose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • maple syrup of choice

Steps

  1. In a bowl add flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the egg and milk and melted butter. Mix until smooth.
  2. Heat an oiled griddle on medium-high heat. Pour the batter on, 1/4 a cup per pancake. Brown on both sides
  3. Serve hot with maple syrup and butter and enjoy
